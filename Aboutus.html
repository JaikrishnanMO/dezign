About us

Shymas Honda (Auto Sales) started its journey with Honda Motorcycle and Scooter India Pvt Ltd (HMSI) dealership in the year 2001. We are the first dealer of HMSI in Kerala. Under the leadership of Mr. T.M. Basheer (Managing Director), the dealership gained reputation of being one of the best Sales and Service providers of Honda Two wheelers in Kerala. As a reward for the high service quality and sales performance by Shymas Honda, Honda Two wheelers has awarded the second dealership in Alleppey District to Shymas Riders Honda at Kayamkulam. Currently we have above 2,50,000 delighted customers. Shymas Honda had branches at. Alappuzha, Cherthala, Kayamkulam, Haripad, Mavelikkara, Charummoodu, Chengannur, Ambalappuzha, Edathua, Pattanakkad, Kattanam, Krishnapuram, Pallikuttumma, Kochalummoodu, Muhamma, Best Deal (Pichu Iyer Jn), Best Deal (Kottukulangara) All workshops of Shymas Honda is fully automated with Quick and Quality service. A team of dedicated technicians trained at HMSI are available to provide after-sales service. Shymas Honda offers a wide range of value-added products to its customers to provide a complete solution to their needs. These include Insurance, Finance, Genuine Accessories, Annual Maintenance Package and Extended Warranty, Honda merchandise goods etc for HMSI Vehicles. Shymas Group has also forayed into Construction, Textile and Hospitality industries.
